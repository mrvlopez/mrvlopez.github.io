  • I will treat others as I would like to be treated
  • My work will always be professional in content and presentation
  • I will share my ideas without putting down the ideas of others
  • I will always leave the classroom with a new skill in my arsenal
  • No task is too difficult for me to achieve
  • Students will interact with mutual respect and demonstrate professionalism in their work
  • Assignments submitted will be of the highest standard in presentation, organization, clarity, and punctuality
  • Students will collaborate with meaningful discussions and debates
  • Gaining new skills will be a daily duty
  • Students will develop a winning mentality of not accepting failure
  • Switching to Computer Lab: Teacher says “Exit Routine” and students secure their materials. Teacher says “Proceed” and the first row of students get up and walk out. When the last student in the row reaches the door, the next row of students follows in sequence. The teacher will follow behind and watch as students take seats in the computer lab one row at a time.
  • Computer Logoff: When the teacher says “Sign Out, Execute” students will save their work, close all open apps, log off the computer and collect all their belongings.
  • Active Shooter Drill: Upon hearing the alarm the teacher yells “Shooter Drill” and two pre-selected students secure the classroom doors from the inside. The row of students closest to the windows will close them. All students will line up standing in rows close together away from the doors.
